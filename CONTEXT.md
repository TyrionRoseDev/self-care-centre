# Quiet Ritual

A personal, single-file self-care companion. Originally a Skincare guide; evolving
into a hub that tells you what to do each day across several self-care Routines.

## Language

**Routine**:
One self-care or training domain the app guides you through — currently Skincare and
Workout, with Haircare, Body care and Bath care planned. Each Routine has its own
screens but shares the app's theme, navigation and settings.
_Avoid_: Module, section, plan

**Today**:
The default view that lists everything due *today* across every Routine, in order —
AM skincare, then Workout (on a training day), then PM skincare. Each block has its own
Completion and is tickable independently: the order is a suggestion, not a gate, so a
skipped Workout never blocks marking PM skincare done. The full day is always visible.
_Avoid_: Home, dashboard

**Session** (Workout):
A single training day's prescribed exercises. The unit the Workout routine schedules
onto specific weekdays. A day that is not a Session is a Rest day.
_Avoid_: Workout (too vague — a Session *is* one workout)

**Rest day** (Workout):
A non-training day. Deliberate and part of the plan — recovery is when toning happens,
and it protects hypermobile joints from flaring.
_Avoid_: Off day, skip day

**Move** (Workout):
One exercise within a Session. Written for someone who has *never exercised*, so it
carries not just its sets/reps or hold time, Focus area and Pain swap, but full
self-teaching instructions: setup, numbered how-to, breathing, what it should feel like,
and common beginner mistakes. No jargon, nothing assumed. A Move you can't picture is a
Move that gets skipped.
_Avoid_: Step (reserved for Skincare), rep, drill

**Focus area** (Workout):
A body region the plan prioritises. In priority order: Stomach (core), Arms, Bum.
_Avoid_: Target zone, problem area

**Pain swap** (Workout):
A gentler alternative for a Move, used when a joint hurts or is unstable that day.
Every Move has one — the plan bends around hEDS rather than pushing through pain.
_Avoid_: Modification, regression, easier version

**Progression** (Workout):
The planned increase in difficulty over weeks — more band resistance, more reps, or
longer holds — so a Session keeps challenging the muscle instead of just maintaining.
_Avoid_: Overload, levelling up

**Completion**:
A single tap marking one Routine block done for the day — `Workout done`,
`Skincare AM done`, `Skincare PM done`, plus an optional daily `Protein ✔` (a protein
shake — no food changes). The whole block, not each Move or step. The Protein tick is
bonus and never blocks a Streak on its own.
_Avoid_: Check-off, log entry

**Check-in**:
The once-nightly, always-optional capture of the day's body signals — Steps, Sleep,
and an optional Feel score (1–5, how the day felt in the body). A bonus like the
Protein tick: checking in can earn Achievements, but a missed Check-in or a low-step
day never breaks a Streak — the numbers are information, not judgement.
_Avoid_: Log, journal, entry, diary

**Tracker**:
The view that turns Completions into feedback — current Streak, weekly consistency, and
earned Achievements.
_Avoid_: History, stats, journal

**Streak**:
Consecutive days (or scheduled days) with the expected Completions met. The main
momentum signal.
_Avoid_: Chain, run

**Achievement**:
An earned milestone that celebrates consistency, not weight or looks — e.g. "first full
week", "10 Sessions done", "4-week streak".
_Avoid_: Badge, trophy, reward
